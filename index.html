Figma

Figma is a cloud-based design tool that enables teams to create, collaborate, and deliver user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) designs in real-time. It offers a range of features, including design files, prototyping, and dev mode, to streamline the design-to-development process.

WordPress

WordPress is a free, open-source content management system (CMS) that allows users to create and manage digital content, including websites, blogs, and e-commerce sites. With thousands of customizable themes, plugins, and widgets, WordPress offers a flexible and user-friendly platform for creating online presence.

Photoshop

Photoshop is a powerful image editing and manipulation software that allows users to enhance, retouch, and transform images, as well as create complex designs and artwork. With its vast array of features, including layers, filters, and effects, Photoshop has become the industry standard for professional photographers, designers, and artists.
